Party. Tonight. 23rd February

February 23 2010, 12:44 AM  by Lloyd Davis

It's one week today till we start our trip in Boston.

We're having a party this evening from 5.30pm until we get tired and need to tidy up.  Everyone's welcome, but please sign up here to let us know you're coming.  We're at the Centre for Creative Collaboration, 16 Acton St, London WC1X 9NG - y'know the place we've been doing Tuttle for a few weeks.

There'll be ukulele, poetry, folk guitar, something we don't really know about yet, and other things that will no doubt be entertaining but haven't yet been confirmed.  Come and do your thing and if you have anything you'd like to donate as a raffle prize that'll get people buying tickets, do let us know and bring it along.

No matter what, in potluck style, please bring something to nibble on, something to swig and someone with deep pockets and a weakness for giving money to starving social artists.
