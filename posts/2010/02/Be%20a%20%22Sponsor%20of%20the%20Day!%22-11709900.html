Be a "Sponsor of the Day!"

February 15 2010, 1:04 AM  by Lloyd Davis

We have a sponsor of the day package.

There are 18 full days that we'll be in the States.  So here's the offer.

As sponsor of the day you get:

  • A blog post before the trip announcing your involvement and describing what you do, how hot/cool/fancy/delicious you are, why people should buy or use your stuff.
  • A blog post on the day reminding our readers/viewers/listeners of your support and how awesome you are.
  • A video blog post of us using, consuming, looking at or talking about your product (and, naturally, it's complete awesomeness) on the day
  • Credits in all content produced on the day ("This post/video/audio was created on Brand X day")
  • If you have branded t-shirts, badges or other clothing and can get it to us in time we will wear it all day long (if it's really nice, some of us may sleep in it).
  • Attendance at a special supporters party on our return including the first screening of our documentary.

We hope that you'll understand that you don't get editorial control of other content posted during the day - we're looking to work with people who know they can trust us to do the right thing.

All for £1,250 (£1,750 for the 5 days we're actually attending SXSW - 12th-16th March)

And you can take as many days as you like and you can choose the day that you sponsor.  First come, first served - ie the earlier you confirm, the wider choice of days you'll have.  The itinerary is on the wiki.  Details of the latter part of the trip will be confirmed shortly.
