Celebrate St Pat's the Muff-a-lotta way in NOLA!

March 13 2010, 2:14 PM  by Brian Condon

IMG_0071.mov Watch this video

I also recorded a series of Audioboo - which I'm embedding here. 

In the first, everyone is getting ready - the Dance Troupe are tense, some nervous, all getting 'psyched-up' for the performance; concentrating on the practicalities of getting ready.  Much laughter and joking to cover the nervousness.

In the second, we're assembling and on the parade - I was pulling the float with Graham; we had technical problems which had to be worked on by a couple of the 'Bun Warmers' (male members of the troupe) as the float moved along at a brisk walking pace.  The dancers' adrenaline was flowing and the energy high as they danced - you can see this in the video above.

In the third, made this morning in Houston TX, I wanted to capture how the crowd reacted to the Muff-a-lottas and how it felt to be part of it.
